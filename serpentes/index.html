Game Overview

Serpentes is a modern twist on the classic snake game, brought to life by Punkcake Délicieux. You control a pixel snake that grows by collecting orbs, but there's a twist—you can also shoot! Navigate a confined arena, avoid self-collisions, blast through obstacles, and stay alive as long as possible. The music is pulsing, the style is retro, and the pressure is on.
